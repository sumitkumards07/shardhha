This banner has a very distinctive identity. It doesn't feel like a typical ecommerce store—it feels like an **editorial luxury fashion brand**. The design language is inspired by heritage, craftsmanship, and understated elegance rather than promotions or discounts.

Here is a detailed design prompt you can use for AI website builders (Lovable, Bolt, v0, Cursor, Replit AI, Figma AI) or as a design brief for developers.

---

# Luxury Heritage Fashion Website Prompt

## Brand Vision

Design a premium Shopify website for a women's ethnic fashion brand named **Thread of Tradition**. The brand sells luxurious kurtis, sarees, festive wear, and handcrafted ethnic clothing.

The website should evoke timeless Indian craftsmanship blended with modern minimalism. Every section should feel calm, sophisticated, and editorial—like a premium fashion magazine rather than a marketplace.

The experience should communicate elegance, authenticity, heritage, premium quality, and emotional storytelling.

Avoid bright ecommerce colors, heavy gradients, excessive banners, popups, or discount-focused layouts.

The overall aesthetic should feel comparable to luxury fashion houses.

# UI Principles

Never use bright red sale badges.

Never clutter the homepage.

Avoid unnecessary banners.

Avoid popups on page load.

Focus on storytelling before selling.

Products should feel aspirational rather than transactional.
